About this page

EuroFanClub is an online community for true Eurovision fans. When logged into the community, you can view EuroVision statistics and vote for your favorite songs for the upcoming 2019 contest.


This site contains information about all winers; from the very first contest back in 1956 when the legendary Lys Assia won for Switzerland. A lot has changed in Eurovision history since then, now a days you don't have to place a telephone call to cast your vote and more countries has joined, what has turned out to be the biggest song competition in the world.


The goal of Eurovision Song Contest stays the same; to unite through music.
